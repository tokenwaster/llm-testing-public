There are 7 cups in a row, numbered 1..7. Cup i starts with a ball labelled i (so cup 1 has ball 1, etc.). Apply these operations in order:

1. Swap the contents of cup 2 and cup 1.
2. Swap the contents of cup 3 and cup 2.
3. Swap the contents of cup 3 and cup 2.
4. Rotate every cup's contents one place to the LEFT (the first cup's ball wraps to the last).
5. Swap the contents of cup 3 and cup 4.
6. Swap the contents of cup 1 and cup 5.
7. Swap the contents of cup 4 and cup 3.
8. Rotate every cup's contents one place to the LEFT (the first cup's ball wraps to the last).
9. Swap the contents of cup 2 and cup 6.
10. Swap the contents of cup 4 and cup 7.
11. Swap the contents of cup 2 and cup 3.
12. Rotate every cup's contents one place to the LEFT (the first cup's ball wraps to the last).

After all operations, which CUP holds the ball that started under cup **1** (i.e. ball 1)? Reply with the cup number, e.g. `3`.
