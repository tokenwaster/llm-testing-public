Struck once. Wasted forever.

Physical challenge coins for people who measure their achievements in tokens. Each edition is die-struck in a numbered run, reeded by hand, and shipped in a felted case nobody asked for.

About the mint

WastedToken Mint has been pressing collectible challenge coins since the first time somebody looked at a context window and said "spend it all." We cut our own dies, mix our own alloys, and reed every edge so the coin tells you it is real before you ever look at the face. No two runs are struck from the same die twice — when an edition sells out, the die is defaced and retired.
