About the Mint

WastedToken Mint was founded on a simple belief: that digital culture deserves tangible artifacts. Each of our challenge coins is precision-struck, hand-finished, and issued in limited runs — bridging the gap between blockchain provenance and the weight of real metal in your palm.

Our master engravers use traditional die-sinking techniques augmented by CNC micro-milling to achieve relief depths and surface finishes that no mass-produced token can match. Every coin carries a unique serial number and a verifiable on-chain certificate of authenticity.
