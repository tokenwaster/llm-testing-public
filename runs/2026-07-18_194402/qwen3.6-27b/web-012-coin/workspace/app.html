About the Mint

Founded in the shadow of forgotten vaults, WastedToken Mint crafts limited-edition challenge coins for collectors who understand that true value lies in scarcity, story, and sheer audacity.

Each coin is struck from premium alloys — bronze, silver, gold, and our legendary obsidian black — then hand-finished with raised relief emblems and reeded edges that catch the light like a promise you can hold.

Our mintage runs are strictly limited. Once a run is exhausted, it is never re-struck. What you see here is what exists. Nothing more. Nothing less.

"A coin wasted is a coin earned."
