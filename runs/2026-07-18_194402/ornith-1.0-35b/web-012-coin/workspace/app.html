About the Mint

At WastedToken Mint, we believe that digital culture deserves physical artifacts. Every coin we produce is a tangible piece of blockchain history — minted with precision, finished by hand, and issued in limited quantities.

Our process combines traditional numismatic techniques with cutting-edge metal printing. Each coin undergoes 72 hours of quality control, from the initial die strike to the final protective coating. We use real metals — bronze, silver, gold, and our proprietary obsidian composite — ensuring each piece has genuine weight and worth.

Whether you're a collector, a developer, or someone who appreciates fine craftsmanship, WastedToken coins are designed to be held, examined, and passed down. This isn't just merchandise — it's tangible legacy.
