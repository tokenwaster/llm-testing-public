About the mint

For the
chronically online.

WastedToken Mint is an independent maker of collectible challenge coins. We archive fleeting internet lore in durable metal: a tiny physical receipt for the hours you spent, the rabbit holes you survived, and the lore you were there for.

Every issue is struck in a limited run, individually inspected, and finished with a reeded edge that feels as good as it looks. No points. No blockchain. Just a very real thing you can put in your pocket.
