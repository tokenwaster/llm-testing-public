About WastedToken Mint

Founded in 2019 by a collective of numismatists, cryptographers, and metalworkers, WastedToken Mint bridges the tactile heritage of military challenge coins with the verifiable scarcity of on-chain assets. Every edition is struck in our Denver facility using century-old drop-hammer presses, then laser-etched with a unique cryptographic fingerprint linked to its on-chain provenance.

We don't print tokens. We mint them — in bronze, silver, gold, and obsidian. Each coin carries weight, history, and a denomination that means something. No rug pulls. No vaporware. Just metal you can hold.
