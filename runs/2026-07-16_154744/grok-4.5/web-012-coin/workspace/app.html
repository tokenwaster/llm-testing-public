About the Mint

WastedToken Mint strikes collectible physical challenge coins for collectors who believe a token should weigh something in the hand. Each edition is die-struck, reeded on the edge, and finished with a true metallic polish — not a printed sticker on a blank slug.

Our house emblem rides the obverse in raised relief; the reverse carries the denomination in bold token counts. Limited runs. Honest metals. No vapor.

From bronze starter pieces to deep-black obsidian seasonals, every coin is inspected under raking light before it leaves the press room. If it does not catch a specular flash, it does not ship.
