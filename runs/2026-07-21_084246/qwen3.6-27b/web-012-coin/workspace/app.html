About the Mint

Founded in the shadow of forgotten ledgers, WastedToken Mint is the premier atelier for collectible physical challenge coins. Each coin is precision-minted from premium alloys, hand-finished, and sealed in archival-grade presentation cases.

Our artisans blend centuries-old minting techniques with contemporary design sensibilities. Every coin tells a story — of resilience, of absurdity, of the beautiful futility of collecting things that serve no purpose other than to be admired.

We do not mint for utility. We mint for legacy. Each edition is limited, each strike is numbered, and each coin carries the unmistakable weight of something that was never supposed to exist — and yet, here it is, gleaming in your palm.

"The coin is not the value. The coin is the point."
