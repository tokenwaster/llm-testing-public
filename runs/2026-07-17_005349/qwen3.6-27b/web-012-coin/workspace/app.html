About the Mint

WastedToken Mint was founded on a singular conviction: that the most enduring tokens are those you can hold in your hand. In an age of ephemeral digital promises, we return to the ancient craft of challenge coin minting — each piece precision-struck, individually numbered, and sealed in a tamper-evident capsule.

Our coins are not mere souvenirs. They are artifacts of commitment, struck in precious and base metals alike, each edition limited and each face a testament to the craft. From the first bronze proof to the obsidian dark series, every WastedToken coin carries the weight of intention.

We mint for collectors, for institutions, and for those who understand that true value is something you can feel between your fingers. Each coin is struck on a 200-ton hydraulic press, finished by hand, and authenticated with a laser-engraved serial number on the edge.
