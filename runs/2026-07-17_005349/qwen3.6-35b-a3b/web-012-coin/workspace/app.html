About the Mint

WastedToken Mint was founded on a simple belief: that digital culture deserves physical artifacts. We produce collectible challenge coins — the same tradition that has honored military service, corporate milestones, and community achievements for over a century — now reimagined for the age of tokens and on-chain identity.

Each coin is struck in real metal using traditional minting techniques: die-engraving, high-relief striking, and hand-finishing. We offer bronze, silver, and gold editions, with limited obsidian runs that push the boundaries of modern metalworking.

Every coin comes with a certificate of authenticity, a unique serial number, and a tamper-evident seal. This is not a print-on-demand novelty — it is a coin, in the truest sense.
