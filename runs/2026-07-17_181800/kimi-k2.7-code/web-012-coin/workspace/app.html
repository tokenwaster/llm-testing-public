About the Mint

WastedToken Mint strikes heirloom-grade challenge coins from bronze, silver, gold, and obsidian alloys. Every piece is minted with a reeded edge, raised relief, and a mirror-polished specular finish that catches the light as the coin turns. Collectors call them “the hardest asset in the room.”

Our coins are not currency — they are proof-of-presence. Carry one, trade one, or lock it away. Either way, it is a tangible piece of the WastedToken legacy.
