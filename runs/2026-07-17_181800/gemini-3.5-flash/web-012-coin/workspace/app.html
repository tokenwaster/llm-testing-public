The Mint

At WastedToken Mint, we bridge the gap between digital scarcity and physical permanence. We believe that the achievements, milestones, and culture of the decentralized web deserve to be held in your hand—not just viewed on a screen.

Every single challenge coin we produce is struck from solid metal alloys, featuring deep-relief 3D embossing, precision-reeded edges, and hand-polished finishes. These are not cheap novelties; they are heavy, satisfying artifacts designed to outlast the servers they were inspired by.
