About the Mint

WastedToken Mint is a small‑batch coin house obsessed with the tactile weight of a real challenge coin. No stamped tin, no printed resin — every design is sculpted, deep‑relief tooled, and struck under 150 tons of pressure before hand finishing.

Deep Relief

Emblems are sculpted in true bas‑relief, not etched, so every coin catches light differently depending on the angle it's held.

Reeded Edges

Every strike is finished with a hand‑set reeded edge — the same anti‑counterfeit technique used on minted currency.

Small Batches
