About WastedToken Mint

Founded in 2018, WastedToken Mint has redefined the art of the challenge coin. Each piece is struck from proprietary alloys using century‑old coining presses, then hand‑finished to bring out a depth of relief that catches light like liquid metal. Our coins are not mere tokens — they are pocket‑sized monuments to the moments, missions, and communities that define us. From the warm glow of Bronze to the obsidian mystery of Black, every edition tells a story you can feel in your hand.
