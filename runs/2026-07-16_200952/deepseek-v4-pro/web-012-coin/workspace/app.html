About the Mint

WastedToken Mint crafts heirloom-grade challenge coins for the most discerning collectors. Each piece is struck with uncompromising precision, featuring deep relief embossing, reeded edges, and a lustrous metallic finish that catches light like no other. Our limited editions are numbered, tracked, and never reminted.

From the weight in your palm to the ring of the strike, every WastedToken coin is a statement. Whether you're commemorating a milestone, rewarding excellence, or building a collection that turns heads — our mint delivers tokens that endure.
