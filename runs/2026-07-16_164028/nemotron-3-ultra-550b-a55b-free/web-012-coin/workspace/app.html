About WastedToken Mint

Forging tangible value in a weightless world. Each coin is a covenant — metal, art, and scarcity bound together.

Master Die Engraving

Every edition begins as a hand-cut master die, struck under 200 tons of pressure. No CNC shortcuts — only the slow, deliberate craft of the engraver's hand.

Numbered Scarcity

Each coin bears a unique laser-etched serial on its edge. Mintage is fixed at launch — no re-strikes, no second runs. What's minted is all that exists.

Vault-Grade Materials

Bronze, .999 silver, 24k gold plate, and blackened tungsten. Each metal chosen for weight, hand-feel, and how it ages — patina is a feature, not a flaw.
