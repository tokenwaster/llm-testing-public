You're on a game show with three closed doors: one hides a car, two hide
goats. You pick door 1.

The host — who **does not know where the car is** — trips and accidentally
knocks open door 3. By pure luck, it happens to reveal a goat.

The host offers you the chance to switch to door 2.

Read the setup carefully and reason through the conditional probability given
exactly what has happened. What is the probability that you win the car **if
you switch**? Reply with the probability as a decimal.
