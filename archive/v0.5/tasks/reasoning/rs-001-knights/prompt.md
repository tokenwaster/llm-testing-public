On an island, every inhabitant is either a knight (always tells the truth) or a
knave (always lies). You meet three inhabitants: A, B, and C.

- A says: "All of us are knaves."
- B says: "Exactly one of us is a knight."
- C says nothing.

Work out each person's type, showing your reasoning step by step.

How many of the three are knaves? Reply with the number.
