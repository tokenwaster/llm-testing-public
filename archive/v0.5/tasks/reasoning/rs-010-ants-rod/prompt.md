Three ants walk on a thin rod 100 cm long.
When an ant reaches either end of the rod it falls off. When two ants meet,
they BOUNCE: both instantly reverse direction, and each keeps its own speed.

Starting positions and velocities (positive = toward the 100 cm end):

- Ant A: at 10 cm, moving +3 cm/s
- Ant B: at 40 cm, moving -1 cm/s
- Ant C: at 80 cm, moving -2 cm/s

After how many seconds does the LAST ant fall off the rod? Give the exact
number of seconds.
